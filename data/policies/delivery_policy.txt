Delivery Policy

1. Orders delayed by more than 60 minutes from the promised delivery time may qualify for compensation.

2. If an order is marked delivered but the customer reports it as not received, verify delivery proof before refund.

3. Missing items should trigger either:
- Replacement, if available and operationally feasible
- Partial refund, if replacement is not possible

4. Repeated delivery issues for the same customer within a short period should be reviewed by delivery operations.

5. Safety-related delivery incidents involving delivery partners must be escalated immediately.
