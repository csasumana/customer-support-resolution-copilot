Escalation Policy

Escalate the ticket if any of the following conditions are true:

1. Fraud or abuse is suspected.
2. Payment disputes exceed 100 USD.
3. There are repeated complaints from the same customer across multiple recent orders.
4. The issue involves safety, harassment, or misconduct by delivery staff.
5. The customer explicitly threatens legal action or regulatory complaint.
6. The ticket cannot be resolved using standard refund, replacement, or apology workflows.
