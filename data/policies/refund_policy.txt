Refund Policy

1. Customers are eligible for a full refund if:
- The order was not delivered.
- Payment was charged but the order failed.
- The wrong item was delivered and replacement is not feasible.

2. Customers are eligible for a partial refund if:
- One or more items are missing from the order.
- Delivered items were damaged but some usable items remain.

3. Refund requests must be made within 30 days of the original order date.

4. Refunds should be processed within 5 business days after approval.

5. If the issue can be resolved through replacement faster than refund, replacement may be preferred for customer satisfaction.

6. High-value refund disputes above 100 USD must be escalated to the payments or risk team.
